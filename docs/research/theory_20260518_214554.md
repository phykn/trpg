# Theory Cycle 14

## Claim

`MeaningContinuity` and `AgencyContinuity` are separate minimum conditions.

## Evidence

Meaning can survive while agency fails:

- A tabletop GM remembers all obligations, motives, injuries, and risks, but blocks every player plan until the intended scene happens.
- A computer RPG keeps lore and quest consequences coherent, but all dialogue choices route to the same outcome.
- Interactive fiction keeps the room and mystery coherent, but gives no usable affordances.
- An LLM maintains continuity but keeps refusing actions without legible constraints or alternatives.

Agency can survive while meaning fails:

- A tactical RPG gives clear choices and reliable combat consequences, but a dead villain returns without explanation.
- Interactive fiction accepts commands and responds clearly, but forgets that the bridge burned down.
- An LLM resolves player plans visibly, but forgets the party's debt, danger, or promise.

## Objection

The current term `MeaningContinuity` is too broad. It can absorb agency because player intent is also part of what makes the fiction meaningful.

The phrase "choices affect outcomes" also overstates agency. A valid failed attempt can preserve agency even when the outcome does not change, as long as the declared intent enters a fair and legible resolution process.

## Revision

Rename and narrow the middle condition:

```text
PlayableContinuity =
TransitionValidity + FictionContinuity + AgencyContinuity
```

- `FictionContinuity`: the accepted next scene remains connected to prior fiction, obligations, risks, world state, and events.
- `AgencyContinuity`: the player can form an actionable intent, identify viable affordances, and trust that declared intent enters the resolution process.

## Why it is stronger

The revision makes the conditions easier to falsify:

- Did the world remember the relevant fiction?
- Did the player have a legible action channel?
- Did the system process the declared intent under visible or inferable constraints?

It also avoids treating coherent railroading as playable continuity.

## Next question

Is `TransitionValidity` independent from `FictionContinuity`, or is validity only observable through whether the fiction explains the transition?
