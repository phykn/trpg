# Cycle 7: Knowledge as Authoritative Proposition

## Claim

`Knowledge` should not be a first-class primitive in `GroundedAffordanceState`.

It should be classified by effect:

- if it changes grounded affordances, represent it under Binding, Commitment, Relation, or another existing primitive
- if it only helps narration, keep it in `ContinuityContext`

## Evidence

- `server/src/game/domain/graph/models.py:6-18` includes `knowledge` as a node type.
- `server/src/game/domain/graph/models.py:42-48` includes `has_knowledge` as an edge type.
- `server/src/game/domain/graph/validation.py:24-30` validates `has_knowledge` from character, item, location, or quest to knowledge.
- `server/tests/game/seed/test_graph_seed.py:669-724` verifies seed graph builds knowledge nodes and `has_knowledge` edges from locations, items, and NPCs.
- `server/tests/game/seed/test_seed_validation.py:342-381` validates knowledge references.
- `server/src/game/runtime/narration/context.py:326-329` includes public knowledge in target view.
- `server/src/game/runtime/narration/context.py:496-514` emits only public knowledge payloads.
- A search over runtime, engines, and LLM context found knowledge use mostly in narration context and content loading, not current action dispatch, roll, or affordance calculation.

## Objection

The claim is too dispatch-centered.

Knowledge can constrain play without changing action dispatch. It constrains what the world can truthfully reveal, answer, or make true in narration.

It does not fit Binding, Commitment, or Relation:

- Binding maps language to concrete ids/actions.
- Commitment represents unresolved goals or obligations.
- Relation represents social/rule state between actors or factions.
- Knowledge represents authoritative propositions attached to world entities.

Additional evidence:

- `server/src/locale/prompts/graph_narrate/prompt.ko.md:147-154` says NPC direct speech must answer only within `target_view`, `target_view.public_knowledge`, `target_view.available_items`, `related_memory`, current place, and current result.
- `server/tests/game/runtime/test_graph_narration_context.py:300-324` verifies public knowledge enters `target_view.public_knowledge` while hidden knowledge is excluded from the payload.

## Revision

Add `Knowledge` as a first-class primitive when it is authoritative propositional state.

Revised definition:

```text
Knowledge = authoritative revealable proposition
```

`Knowledge` belongs to `GroundedAffordanceState` when it constrains grounded dialogue, investigation, reveal, or truth-bound narration.

Pure flavor knowledge remains `ContinuityContext`.

## Why It Is Stronger

The revision extends grounded play beyond mechanical dispatch.

It explains why public knowledge is graph-linked, validated, visibility-filtered, and exposed in narration payloads even though it does not currently drive action dispatch.
